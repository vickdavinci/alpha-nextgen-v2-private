## 8) Optimization Recommendations

### P0 — Critical
1. **Drawdown Governor stuck at 0% for ~80 days**  
   Evidence: `EQUITY_RECOVERY: Day 80 at 0%` on 2022-06-30.  
   Impact: System largely sidelined, poor recovery.  
   Fix: Lower recovery threshold or add automatic regime override after X days at 0%.

2. **Options blocked by KS Tier 1 for long stretches**  
   Evidence: 70 `OPTIONS_EOD: Blocked by KS Tier 1 (REDUCE)` lines.  
   Impact: Options engine shut down for most of the period.  
   Fix: Reduce KS Tier 1 sensitivity for options, or allow bearish spreads in REDUCE.

### P1 — High
1. **Spread performance in early Jan caused most of losses**  
   Evidence: 5 spreads, 0% win rate, -$10,056 net.  
   Impact: >85% of total P&L loss.  
   Fix: Tighten regime gate for bullish spreads or use smaller sizing until regime confirms.

2. **Regime spent only ~9% in RISK_OFF during bear market**  
   Evidence: Regime distribution in logs.  
   Impact: Too many neutral/bullish allocations into drawdown.  
   Fix: Increase drawdown weight or tighten thresholds for CAUTIOUS/DEFENSIVE transitions.

### P2 — Medium
1. **Spread margin blocks at 20-spread size**  
   Evidence: 2 `SPREAD: BLOCKED - Insufficient margin`.  
   Impact: Missed bearish opportunities.  
   Fix: Adaptive sizing for spreads before margin check to avoid full block.

2. **Hedge engine losses**  
   Evidence: 7 hedge trades, 0% win rate, -$1,412 net.  
   Impact: Hedges dragged equity lower.  
   Fix: Tighten hedge activation to lower regimes or add duration stop for TMF/PSQ.

### P3 — Low
1. **Missing timeline logs (Time Guard, MR force close)**  
   Evidence: 0 log matches.  
   Impact: Harder to audit event correctness.  
   Fix: Log only once/day for these events to preserve log budget.

## 10) Bottom Line
The poor performance is primarily driven by **early‑January option spread losses** and a **drawdown governor that clamps the system at 0% for most of the half‑year**, preventing recovery trades.
