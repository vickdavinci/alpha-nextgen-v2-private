## 7. Key Findings

### Strengths

1. **Capital Protection:** Lost only 9.8% vs Nasdaq -30% (20%+ outperformance)
2. **Governor System:** 3-tier system correctly scaled down exposure
3. **Defensive Mode:** At Governor 0%, only hedges/PUTs allowed - worked correctly
4. **VIX Direction:** Successfully integrated into daily regime scoring
5. **MR Discipline:** Correctly blocked during unsuitable conditions
6. **Kill Switch Unused:** Graduated governor response prevented need for nuclear option

### Areas for Improvement

1. **Options Win Rate (30.7%):** Below target of 40%+. Consider:
   - Tighten entry criteria during high VIX
   - Reduce position size further when VIX > 25
   - Investigate VASS rejection patterns

2. **TMF Hedge Ineffective:** Rising rates environment made 3x Treasury hedge counterproductive. Consider:
   - Adding interest rate regime awareness
   - Alternative hedge instruments (TAIL, UVXY for volatility spikes)
   - Conditional hedge selection based on rate environment

3. **Spread Construction:** Many VASS rejections in high VIX. Consider:
   - Widening delta/credit criteria during crisis
   - Alternative spread widths for high IV
   - Logging more detail on rejection reasons

4. **HWM Stale:** HWM stayed at $51,341 for 5+ months. Consider:
   - Time-based HWM decay for multi-month bear markets
   - Regime override for HWM when in strong bull (already in config)
