   - **Evidence:** `EXERCISE_DETECTED` and `EXERCISE_LIQUIDATE` in April.  
   - **Impact:** Large forced liquidations; catastrophic risk.  
   - **Fix:** Review early‑exercise guard and ensure no short ITM exposure near expiry.

### P1 — High

   - **Evidence:** 49 REDUCE + 74 options blocked.  
   - **Impact:** Options trading suppressed during volatility.  
   - **Fix:** Regime‑aware KS thresholds or cooldown for options blocking.

4) **Trend engine losses dominate**  
   - **Evidence:** Equities net **-$40,170** vs options **+$599**.  
   - **Impact:** Portfolio drawdown driven by trend, not options.  
   - **Fix:** Tighten trend exposure in CAUTIOUS/DEFENSIVE, or disable trend for options‑only validation.

### P2 — Medium
5) **VASS daily tracking logs missing**  
   - **Evidence:** No `VASS.*5d.*20d` logs.

   - **Fix:** Add explicit logging to confirm 5d/20d values.

6) **Position limit enforcement not logged**
