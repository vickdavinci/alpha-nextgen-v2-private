## 8) Recommendations

### **P0 — Critical**
1) **Governor death-spiral (0% for 160 days)**  
   - **Evidence:** `2022-01-22 … Scale 50% → 0%` and 160 days at 0%  
   - **Impact:** Eliminated recovery participation; forced prolonged defensive-only posture  
   - **Fix:** Rework governor recovery logic or raise shutdown threshold for bear-trend markets.

### **P1 — High**
2) **Options engine drawdown dominating**  
   - **Evidence:** Options net **-$23,739**; spread win rate **20.2%**  
   - **Impact:** Core driver of -50.6% total return  
   - **Fix:** Tighten entry filtering; increase credit/defensive bias in high-VIX; reduce quantity scaling.

3) **VASS routing mismatch at high VIX**  
   - **Evidence:** VIX 28–29 still produced **DEBIT** spreads  
   - **Impact:** Missed premium advantage during elevated vol  
   - **Fix:** Validate VASS routing thresholds and ensure credit mode above VIX 25.

### **P2 — Medium**
4) **Excessive VASS rejections (1,929)**  
   - **Evidence:** Persistent `VASS_REJECTION` logs in Jan/Feb  
   - **Impact:** Signal-to-execution leakage  
   - **Fix:** Review DTE/delta constraints or reduce strictness in medium IV.

5) **Kill switch frequency (13 REDUCE triggers)**  
   - **Evidence:** `KS_GRADUATED: NONE → REDUCE` 13 times  
   - **Impact:** Frequent reduction adds churn and limits recovery  
   - **Fix:** Consider regime-based KS thresholds or volatility-adjusted limits.

### **P3 — Low**
6) **Missing PRE_MARKET / TIME_GUARD logs**  
   - **Evidence:** No matching entries in log  
   - **Impact:** Reduced auditability  
   - **Fix:** Ensure critical lifecycle logs are `trades_only=True` where needed.
