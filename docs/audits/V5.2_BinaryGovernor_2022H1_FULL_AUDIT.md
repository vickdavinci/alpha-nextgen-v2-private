## STEP 11: Recommendations

### P0 — CRITICAL

**1. Governor Threshold Too Late**
- **What:** 15% DD threshold triggers after significant damage already done
- **Evidence:** $18,000+ lost on CALL options before Feb 5 trigger
- **Impact:** Earlier trigger (10-12%) would have saved $5,000-10,000
- **Fix:** `DRAWDOWN_GOVERNOR_LEVELS = {0.10: 0.00}` or add early warning at 10%

**2. Recovery Conditions Impossible in Prolonged Bear**
- **What:** DD must fall below 12%, regime >= 60 for 5 days — never happened
- **Evidence:** 147 days stuck at 0% with no recovery attempt
- **Impact:** System fully decapacitated for 81% of backtest
- **Fix:** Add time-based partial recovery (after 30 days at 0%, allow 25% capacity)

### P1 — HIGH

**3. CALL Options Traded in Declining Market**
- **What:** CALL spreads entered at regime 60-68 during January decline
- **Evidence:** -$17,341 losses in UPPER_NEUTRAL trades
- **Impact:** Major P&L drag
- **Fix:** Raise CALL spread threshold to 70+ only, or add trend confirmation

**4. PUT Spreads Not Profitable**
- **What:** 488 PUT trades allowed but most closed at tiny losses
- **Evidence:** CAUTIOUS regime trades: -$1,250 on 18 trades
- **Impact:** Bear market not captured
- **Fix:** Review PUT spread strike selection and profit targets

**5. TMF Hedge Ineffective**
- **What:** TMF (3× Treasury bonds) declined -13% in rising rate environment
- **Evidence:** 4 TMF trades, -$745 total loss
- **Impact:** Hedge added to losses instead of reducing
- **Fix:** Replace TMF with TLT (less volatile) or disable in rising rate regime

### P2 — MEDIUM

**6. VASS Rejection Rate Too High**
- **What:** 1,920 rejections due to spread criteria
- **Evidence:** "No contracts met spread criteria (DTE/delta/credit)"
- **Fix:** Widen delta tolerances or minimum credit requirements

**7. Margin Errors**
- **What:** 10 orders rejected for insufficient buying power
- **Evidence:** Position sizing not adjusting for reduced equity
- **Fix:** Add equity-based position cap in sizing logic

## Conclusion

The V5.2 Binary Governor **works as designed** — it correctly triggered at 15% DD and stayed defensive. However, the design has three critical flaws in a prolonged bear market:

1. **Threshold too late:** 15% trigger allows significant pre-governor losses
2. **Recovery impossible:** DD/regime/equity requirements too strict
3. **PUT spreads ineffective:** Defensive mode allowed PUTs but they didn't profit

**Recommended Changes for V5.3:**
1. Lower governor threshold to 10-12%
2. Add time-based recovery (partial capacity after 30+ days at 0%)
3. Review PUT spread parameters for bear market profitability
4. Replace or disable TMF hedge in rising rate environments
