## Step 9) Optimization Recommendations

### P0 — CRITICAL
1. **Regime misclassification in bull market**  
   **Evidence:** 0 days classified as RISK_ON in 2017.  
   **Impact:** Bull regime never enabled full long posture; exposure was constrained while options losses dominated.  
   **Fix:** Re‑calibrate regime thresholds or factor scaling to allow RISK_ON in sustained bull environments.

2. **Excessive slippage on options (246 events)**  
   **Evidence:** 246 `SLIPPAGE_EXCEEDED` logs.  
   **Impact:** Likely material drag on options P&L and execution quality.  
   **Fix:** Tighten entry filters for illiquid contracts, widen bid‑ask checks, or cap trade size during low liquidity windows.

### P1 — HIGH
1. **Options engine dominates losses**  
   **Evidence:** Options net P&L -$36,333; spread win rate 14.4%.  
   **Impact:** Overwhelms positive trend performance.  
   **Fix:** Reduce options allocation in NEUTRAL regime or require higher conviction signals.

2. **Governor and KS events frequent despite bull year**  
   **Evidence:** KS_GRADUATED=59; Governor hits 0% on 2017‑03‑22.  
   **Impact:** De‑risking in bull year lowers participation and compounds losses.  
   **Fix:** Tune drawdown/KS thresholds for low‑volatility bull environments.

### P2 — MEDIUM
1. **Trend entries blocked by position limits**  
   **Evidence:** 51 `ENTRY_BLOCKED` events.  
   **Impact:** Limits diversification in trend engine.  
   **Fix:** Review max position count or enable rotation logic.
