**Root Cause Analysis — Why VASS Was Completely Blocked:**

2024 was a persistently low-VIX bull market (VIX average ~15.7 at VASS rejection events, range 11.8 to 38.5). This created a double-bind:

- **Bearish direction** (BEAR_PUT_DEBIT): Blocked by `R_BEAR_PUT_ASSIGNMENT_GATE_LOW_VIX_RELAXED` (52.6% of all drops). In low-VIX environments, the relaxed assignment gate requires the short PUT to be further OTM than available strikes, eliminating all viable BEAR_PUT candidates. Additionally, `R_BEAR_PUT_RISK_ON_BLOCK` (54 drops) blocked BEAR_PUT in RISK_ON/NEUTRAL regime.

- **Bullish direction** (BULL_CALL_DEBIT): Blocked by `R_CONTRACT_QUALITY_THETA_BURN_TOO_HIGH` (118 drops) — when VIX is low (~12-15), the premium for bull call debit spreads is thin, causing the theta-to-debit ratio to blow out far above the 8% threshold. Additionally, `R_BULL_DEBIT_TREND_MA20` (116 drops) blocked BULL_CALL entries when QQQ pulled back intraday below the MA20 trend band.

- **Both directions simultaneously blocked** for most of Q1 2024: VASS attempted entries every 15 minutes but was rejected continuously, as shown in log lines 571-627 (Jan 10 sample showing 18+ consecutive rejections in a single day).

## Recommendations

### High Priority (Critical)

**1. Fix VASS Bear Put Assignment Gate for Low VIX**
- Root cause: `R_BEAR_PUT_ASSIGNMENT_GATE_LOW_VIX_RELAXED` blocked 708 of 1,347 VASS candidates (52.6%)
- In 2024's low-VIX environment (avg 15.7), the relaxed gate required short PUT legs further OTM than available strikes
- Fix: Widen acceptable OTM band when VIX < 16, or reduce minimum OTM distance from 2% to 1% in LOW IV env
- Expected impact: Potentially 300-500 VASS BEAR_PUT trades unlocked per year

**2. Fix VASS Bull Debit Theta Burn Threshold**
- `THETA_BURN_TO_DEBIT` threshold (8%) is unattainable when VIX < 16 (observed values: 68% - 252%)
- Fix: Make threshold adaptive to VIX level (e.g., 8% at VIX>20, 30% at VIX<16), or switch primary filter to debit-to-width ratio
- Expected impact: Unlocks BULL_CALL_DEBIT in 116 blocked scenarios (exact from crosscheck: R_BULL_DEBIT_TREND_MA20=116)

**3. Tighten ITM Hard Stop to Reduce Tail Loss Concentration**
- 9 of 32 ITM trades hit stops in -$3,180 to -$3,950 range, generating 54% of all loss dollars
- Fix: Tighten OCO stop from current implied 45% stop (config ITM_STOP_PCT) to a tighter level, or reduce contracts from 5-6 to 3-4
- Expected impact: Compress tail from -$3,700 avg to -$2,200 avg (estimated -$13,500 loss reduction)

**4. Add FOMC Date Blackout for ITM Entries**
- Dec 17-18 FOMC hawkish surprise caused -$7,235 in two consecutive stops
- Fix: Block new ITM CALL entries within 1 trading day before and after FOMC announcement dates
- Expected impact: Avoids the single largest 2-day loss event in the year

### Medium Priority

**5. September 2024 MICRO Reversal Guard**
- 26 trades at 25% win rate in September when QQQ reversed sharply after sell-off
- Fix: Add a VIX reversal guard — if VIX spikes >10% intraday then falls >20% same session, pause PROTECTIVE_PUTS for the day
- Expected impact: ~$4,000-$6,000 recovery on September-type scenarios

**6. Expand ITM Concurrent Cap from 1 to 2**
- R_ITM_CONCURRENT_CAP generated ~1,800 drops — the slot cap (1 position) was frequently occupied by a slow-exiting trade
- Fix: Allow 2 concurrent ITM positions with proportional size reduction (2.5 contracts each instead of 5)
- Expected impact: Approximately 2x ITM trade frequency while maintaining same dollar exposure

**7. Review May 2024 Dead Zone**
- May had zero trades despite healthy market conditions
- Cause: MICRO blocked by CAUTION_LOW + ITM slot occupied by prior open position
- Fix: Ensure MICRO can activate independently even when ITM slot is occupied; they share different slot types

**8. VASS Signal Generation Cost Reduction**
- With 0/1347 fill rate, VASS wastes significant compute generating candidates that always fail
- Fix: Add a daily VASS circuit breaker — if 3 consecutive days have 0 VASS fills, reduce scan frequency from every-15-min to hourly until conditions change
- Expected impact: Reduced log budget pressure and compute overhead
