**The TAIL_RISK_CAP fires at $1,000 loss on a position. With 15 contracts at ~$3.50-4.00 average debit = ~$5,250 notional, the cap triggers at 19% of cost basis.**

The cap is providing no benefit — it's simply a harder stop that fires at a level too tight for the natural price volatility of these spreads.

Post-election QQQ volatility regime caught the system with maximum VASS exposure. 5 of 6 November spreads were losses.
