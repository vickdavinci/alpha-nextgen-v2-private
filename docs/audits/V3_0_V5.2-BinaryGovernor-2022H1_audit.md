## 8) Recommendations

### **P0 — Critical**
1) **Governor never re-armed (146 days at 0%)**  
   - **Evidence:** Defensive mode on 2022-02-05; no recovery/guard pass through 2022-06-30  
   - **Impact:** Recovery participation blocked for entire H1  
   - **Fix:** Review recovery thresholds (DD < 12%, regime guard 60×5d, equity recovery 5%/7d) to ensure re-arming is achievable during prolonged bear conditions.

### **P1 — High**
2) **Options engine drawdown dominates**  
   - **Evidence:** Options net **-$32,087**; spread win rate **21.2%**  
   - **Impact:** Primary driver of -66% net return  
   - **Fix:** Tighten entry filters, reduce sizing during CAUTIOUS/DEFENSIVE, and validate bear‑mode structures.

3) **VASS selection too strict in high VIX**  
   - **Evidence:** 1,920 VASS rejections; CREDIT strategy attempted but no contracts selected  
   - **Impact:** Missed premium opportunities during high IV regimes  
   - **Fix:** Relax delta/DTE/credit thresholds for credit structures in high VIX.

### **P2 — Medium**
4) **Frequent KS REDUCE (12)**  
   - **Evidence:** `KS_GRADUATED: NONE → REDUCE` 12 times  
   - **Impact:** Churn and reduced exposure during volatile swings  
   - **Fix:** Consider regime-aware KS thresholds or volatility-adjusted tiers.

### **P3 — Low**
5) **Missing PRE_MARKET / TIME_GUARD logs**  
   - **Evidence:** No matching entries in log  
   - **Impact:** Audit visibility reduced  
   - **Fix:** Ensure critical lifecycle logs are `trades_only=True`.
